The International Conference on Technlogy for Humanity is an event based on the current global situations at hand. As the world has improved greatly in terms of technological advances and has moved further into the future of an "automated world", many challenges and issues have arisen. But with every issue comes a solution or potential solution that is fashioned specifically for that problem.
